- All IPC handlers live in `src/main/ipc-handlers.ts` ŌĆö new IPC channels must be registered there and added to `removeIpcHandlers()` cleanup
- Per-bot lifecycle resources (FSM runner, grid screencast, focus window) are encapsulated in `BotSession` (`src/main/bot-session.ts`) ŌĆö BotRunner uses `Map<string, BotSession>` instead of separate Maps
