Round Structure

You will be rematched every round for {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds.

  1. Read matrix and private-signal information.
  2. Complete two attention checks.
  3. Report your belief about your partner's action.
  4. Choose one discrete action: Stag or Hare.
  5. Wait for your partner and receive results.
Payoff Matrix

The state can be Good or Bad. You do not observe the true state directly. You receive a private signal that is correct with probability {{ C.SIGNAL_ACCURACY }}.
